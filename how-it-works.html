How to Use DIU PDF BOX

Follow this simple step-by-step guide to create professional assignment covers and merge your PDFs in minutes!

1

Fill Document Settings

Start by configuring your document preferences:

  • Document Type: Choose from Assignment, Lab Task, Lab Report or Quiz
  • Output File Name: Enter your desired PDF filename (e.g., "CSE101_Assignment.pdf")
Tip: Use descriptive filenames like "CourseCode_AssignmentName.pdf" for better organization
2

Enter Academic Information

Provide your semester and submission details:

  • Semester: Enter current semester (e.g., "Spring 2024")
  • Submit Date: Click the calendar icon to select your submission date
Note: The date will automatically format to DD/MM/YY for consistency
3

Add Student Details

Fill in your personal information:

  • Student Name: Your full name as registered in DIU
  • Student ID: Your DIU student ID number
  • Batch: Your batch number (e.g., "55")
  • Section: Your section (e.g., "A", "B", "C")
4

Course Information

Enter details about your course:

  • Course Code: The official course code (e.g., "CSE101")
  • Course Name: Full course title (e.g., "Programming Fundamentals")
  • Teacher Name: Your instructor's name
  • Designation: Teacher's position (e.g., "Professor", "Lecturer")
5

Upload Your Files

Add your assignment content:

  • Drag & Drop: Simply drag files into the upload area
  • Click to Browse: Click the upload area to select files
  • Supported Formats: Images (JPG, PNG) and PDF files
  • Reorder: Drag uploaded files to change their order
Pro Tip: You can reorder files by dragging them up or down in the list
6

Generate & Download

Create your final PDF:

  • Click the "Generate PDF & Preview" button
  • Wait for processing (usually takes a few seconds)
  • Preview your generated PDF
  • Download the final document to your device
Success! Your professional DIU assignment is ready for submission

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats are supported?

DIU PDF BOX supports JPG, PNG images and PDF files. You can mix different formats in a single document.

Is there a file size limit?

For optimal performance, we recommend keeping individual files under 10MB. The tool works entirely in your browser.

Do I need to create an account?

No! DIU PDF BOX is completely free and requires no registration. Just visit the site and start creating.

Can I use this on my phone?

Absolutely! DIU PDF BOX is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets.

Are my files stored anywhere?

No, all processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.
