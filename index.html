üöÄ How It Works

Creating professional DIU documents is now super simple! Follow these easy steps:

Fill Information

Enter your student details, course info and select document type (Assignment/Lab Report)

Upload Files

Drag & drop your images or PDFs. Reorder them by dragging to arrange as needed

Generate PDF

Click "Generate PDF & Preview" - we'll create a professional cover page automatically

Preview & Download

Check your document in the preview, rename if needed, then download your compressed PDF

üí° Pro Tips

File Size: Images are automatically compressed to keep PDFs small (3-6MB instead of 20MB+)

Mobile: On phones, use "Open PDF" button since preview isn't available

Order: Drag files in the upload section to reorder pages

Formats: Supports JPG, PNG, PDF files - mix and match as needed
